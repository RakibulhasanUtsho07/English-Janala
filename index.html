FrequentlyAsked Questions

1.How can I start learning English on this website?

You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.

2.Is this website free to use?

You can access my core features, including chat and basic image generation, at no cost with a standard Google account. However, there are paid tiers available if you need higher limits or professional tools.

3.Do I need to create an account?

The English Janala app is free to use and does not require an account for its core lessons and features.

4.How can I build my English vocabulary?

You can use my Learning Suite to turn your notes into custom flashcards and interactive quizzes for daily vocabulary practice.

5.Do you offer certificates for completed courses?

No, I provide personalized tutoring and practice tools to help you learn, but I do not issue official certificates for completed courses.
